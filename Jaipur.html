Jaipur


Here are some details about Jaipur, India:
Known as
The Pink City, Jaipur is the capital of Rajasthan and is known for its rose-colored buildings. History
Founded in 1727 by Sawai Jai Singh II, Jaipur was India's first planned city. The city was designed by Vidyadhar Bhattacharya and built using principles of Vastu Shastra.
Planning
Jaipur's grid plan combines ideas from ancient Hindu, early modern Mughal, and Western cultures. The city's streets are lined with shops and businesses, and large public squares called chaupars are created where the streets intersect.
Attractions
Jaipur is home to many attractions, including:
Jantar Mantar: An 18th-century open-air observatory and UNESCO World Heritage site
Hawa Mahal: Also known as the Hall of Winds, this palace has over 900 small windows to help cool the interior
City Palace: Part of the palace is home to the royal family of Jaipur
Nahargarh: Also known as the Tiger Fort
Location
Jaipur is located in the north-western state of Rajasthan, 167 miles from New Delhi.
Tourism
aipur is a popular tourist destination and part of the west Golden triangle tourist circuit with Delhi and Agra.
Transportation
Car rentals are available in Jaipur from companies like Zoomcar, Myles, and Avis.
