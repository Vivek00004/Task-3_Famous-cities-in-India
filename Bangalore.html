Bangalore city(Silicon Valley of India)


Here are some facts about Bangalore, the capital of Karnataka, India:
Location
Bangalore is in the southeast of Karnataka, in the heart of the Mysore Plateau. It's a landlocked city with an average elevation of 920 meters (3,020 ft).
Population
Bangalore is one of India's largest cities, with a population of over 10 million.
Known for
Bangalore is known as the "Silicon Valley of India" and is a popular destination for science, industry, and education. It's also known as a "shoppers' paradise".
Culture
Bangalore is a cultural meeting point for speakers of Kannada, Telugu, and Tamil. It has many historical monuments and offers a variety of cultural and recreational activities.
Climate
Bangalore has a tropical savanna climate with distinct wet and dry seasons.
Transportation
Bangalore has a bus service that starts around 5 AM and ends around midnight. The Kempegowda bus stand is near the Bangalore City railway station. History
Bangalore was founded in the 16th century by Kempegowda I.
Rava idli
Bangalore is the birthplace of the popular Rava idli, which was invented during World War II when rice was in short supply.
Music
Bangalore has many music schools that offer training in Carnatic, Hindustani classical, and Western music.
